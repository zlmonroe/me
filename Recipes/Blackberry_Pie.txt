Blackberry Pie

Ingredients
	5 cups frozen blackberries
	1/2 - 3/4 cup sugar
	1 t lemon zest
	1 t lemon juice
	1/2 t cinnamon
	1/4 t almond extract
	3 Tbs quick cooking instant tapioca

Instructions
	Mix and let sit for 30 minutes
	Preheat oven to 400 degrees
	Cook for 30 min
	Top with aluminum foil
	Reduce to bake 350 degrees
	Cook another 30 minutes
