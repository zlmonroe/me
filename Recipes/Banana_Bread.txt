Williams and Sonoma Banana Bread

Ingredients
	1 2/3 Cups all-purpose flout
	2 tsp baking powder
	½ tsp salt
	¼ tsp baking soda
	3 tsp bananas
	2/3 cups sugar
	1/3 cups vegetable oil
	2 eggs
	1 ½ tsp vanilla
	¼ tsp cinnamon
	1/8 tsp cloves
	pinch of nutmeg

Instructions
	Bake at 350 degrees
