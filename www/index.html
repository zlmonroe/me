Who am I?

My name is Zach Monroe. I am a student, a researcher, an explorer, a photographer, and much more. I am studying at Arizona State University and pursuing a Bachelor's of Science in Software Engineering with a primary focus in Web Development and a secondary focus in Machine Learning. Currently I am researching the applications of Machine Learning towards cryptography and password security. In my spare time, I love to hike and camp; I'm always looking for new places to explore. I also love to photograph my experiences to share with the world. This is my portfolio where I hope to share the experiences I've had with the world!
