Garbanzo Bean Chocolate Cake

Ingredients
	1 and 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
	1 can (19 oz.) garbanzo beans, rinsed and drained
	4 eggs
	3/4 cup white sugar
	1/2 tsp baking powder
	1 Tbsp vanilla extract
	1 Tbsp confectioner's sugar
	Raspberries

Instructions
	1 Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
	2 Grease an 8-inch round cake pan
	3 Melt the chocolate chips in the microwave
	4 Combine the beans and eggs in a food processor, or mix by hand
	5 Add the sugar and baking powder and continue to mix
	6 Pour into pan
	7 Bake for 40 minutes, or until knife comes out clean when inserted.
	8 Cool for 10 to 15 minutes
	9 Invert onto serving plate
	10 Dust with confectioner's sugar and raspberries
