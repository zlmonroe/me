Chicken Souvlakia

Ingredients
	½ cup thinly sliced onion
	2 tablespoons olive oil
	1 ½ tsp fresh lemon juice
	1 ½ tsp finely chopped fresh parsley
	¼ tsp dried oregano
	1 bay leaf
	1 lb. skinless boneless chicken breast cut into ~32 bite size pieces
	½ red onion cut into 8 ~1-inch size pieces
	½ large green bell pepper cut into ~1-inch size pieces
	8 button mushrooms
	8 large cherry tomatoes
	¼ teaspoon salt
	¼ teaspoon black pepper
	Cooking spray

Instructions
	Combine first 6 ingredients in a large zip lock plastic bag, add chicken. Seal and marinate in refrigerator for 3 hours; turn occasionally
	Soak skewers
	Prepare grill
	Add meat and veggies to skewers
	grill ~12 minutes or until chicken comes up to temp, turning once
	Optionally serve with Tzatziki sauce
