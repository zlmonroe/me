Chimichurri Sauce

Ingredients
	4 garlic cloves
	2 cups fresh parsley leaves
	1 cup fresh cilantro leaves
	1 jalapeño stemmed and seeded
	¾ cup extra virgin olive oil
	2 Tbsp lemon juice
	2 Tbsp rice wine vinegar (or red wine vinegar)
	2 tsp dried oregano
	2 tsp ground cumin
	1 tsp salt
	pepper to taste

Instructions
	Mince garlic, parsley, cilantro, and jalapeño in a food processor
	Add olive oil, lemon juice, vinegar, oregano, cumin, salt and pepper
	Mix
