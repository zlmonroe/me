Corn Bread
	
Ingredients
	1 cup all-purpose flour
	1 cup yellow, white, or blue cornmeal
	2 to 4 tablespoons sugar
	1 tablespoon baking powder
	1/2 teaspoon salt
	2 eggs
	1 cup milk
	1/4 cup cooking oil or shortening melted

Instructions
	Mix dry ingredients, mix wet ingredients, mix, then bake at 425 degrees for 20 to 25 minutes or until golden brown
