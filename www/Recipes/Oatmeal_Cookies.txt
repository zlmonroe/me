Oatmeal Cookies

Ingredients
	1 Cup all-purpose flour
	1 tsp baking powder
	½ tsp baking soda
	¼ tsp salt
	2 cups oats
	½ cup chopped nuts (optional)
	8 Tablespoon butter (room temp)
	½ cups lightly packed brown sugar
	½ cups granulated sugar
	1 egg
	¼ cup maple syrup
	½ tsp vanilla
	¾ cup semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions
	Bake at 350 degrees
